Soroush Zare

Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering, University of Virginia, Charlottesville, VA

I am a PhD Candidate in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at the University of Virginia. My research focuses on integrating advanced artificial intelligence with healthcare, designing and controlling innovative products such as soft exoskeletons and robots, and refining brain-computer interfaces (BCI) and enhancing EEG motor imagery classification using CNNs, transformer models, and reinforcement learning.
